Mi 11 India launch confirmed by Xiaomi

27 March, 2021 by Sai Kamal 73
  • Share on Facebook
  • Tweet on Twitter

The Mi 11 India launch has been teased once officially, as Xiaomi tweeted an iamge saying the series is “coming soon.” This confirmation comes two months after the company’s marketing lead first spoke about launching the Mi 11 series in India. To recall, Xiaomi has scheduled a global launch event on March 29th, where it is expected to unveil the Mi 11 Ultra, Mi 11 Lite, and other products. Considering that, the Mi 11 India launch date will likely be in April. Xiaomi hasn’t revealed which phones in series will be launching in the country, though we can expect at least the Lite and the standard models to arrive here. The Mi 11 was originally launched in China last December with the Snapdragon 888 chipset.

Here's what Xiaomi said about the launch:

he teaser by Xiaomi India confirms that the Mi 11 India launch is indeed happening very soon. The company tweeted the teaser soon after the OnePlus 9 series India launch. The OnePlus 9 series, which includes the OnePlus 9R, OnePlus 9, and OnePlus 9 Pro, comes with a starting price of Rs 39,999. The OnePlus 9 and OnePlus 9 Pro come with the Snapdragon 888 SoC and Hasselblad cameras. The upcoming Mi 11 series will take on the OnePlus flagships.

The Mi 11 is powered by the Snapdragon 888 chipset and offers a 108MP camera. If the company prices the phone aggressively in India, then OnePlus is going to have some tough competition.

Mi 11 Specifications

The Mi 11 specifications include the Snapdragon 888 SoC, up to 12GB RAM, 256GB UFS 3.1 storage, and a triple rear camera setup with a 108-megapixel primary sensor. The phone features a 6.81-inch E4 AMOLED display with QHD+ resolution and 120Hz refresh rate. It comes with a 4,600mAh battery that supports 55W wired charging, and 50W wireless charging. It gets an in-display fingerprint scanner, a 20-megapixel selfie camera, Harman Kardon speakers, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, and runs Android 11 based MIUI 12.5 UI. The Mi 11 Ultra is rumoured to come with a secondary display at the back, and improved camera performance. We don’t have to wait long to find out all the details.

Consider before buying

Xiaomi Mi 11 is a gamer's paradise with its Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 chipset, which offers HDR level graphics teamed with desktop level capabilities. Considering the camera options loaded within the device, vloggers will get to shoot multiple contents using a single smartphone. Moreover, Xiaomi ensures prolonged usage by offering a 4600mAh battery with 55W v4.0 Quick Charging and Wireless Charging technology. Not to forget the impeccable Quad cameras led by 108MP main shooter. Xiaomi Mi 11 also portrays a 6.81-inch AMOLED screen with a resolution of 1440 x 3200 pixels along with an aspect ratio of 20:9. The bezel-less screen of the device flaunts a punch-hole layout and is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass v5 protection. It has a pixel density of 515ppi. The device features a triple-camera configuration on its rear side including a 108MP Primary Camera, a 13MP Ultra-Wide Angle Lens and a 5MP Macro Camera. Some of the features flaunted by the rear camera setup include ISO control, Face detection, HDR mode, Digital Zoom and more. There is a 20MP selfie-shooting camera on the front side of the device. Configuration and Battery Xiaomi Mi 11 comes packed with 8GB RAM partnered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 chipset offering variable rate shading feature. An Octa-core processor setup, Kryo 680 single-core, tri-core and quad-core layout functioning at a speed of 2.84GHz, 2.42GHz and 1.8GHz ensure quality performance. An Adreno 660 GPU looks after the graphical needs. The smartphone supports Wi-Fi, mobile hotspot, A-GPS Glonass, USB Type-C, NFC, Bluetooth v5.0. Users also get access to 5G and 4G VoLTE bands.
